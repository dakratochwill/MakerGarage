Pricing and Work Orders

Charging and Invoicing Policies

To maintain the high level of professional technical support that the VCU College of Engineering Maker Garage provides to faculty, staff and students, all machines, tooling, and hardware must be maintained on a regular basis.

Therefore, due to the costs associated with maintaing the shop, it is necessary to charge a fee for work and services provided by our staff and/or technicians. Consulting and design assistance is provided.

Any and all materials used for projects must be purchased by the user either from the shop or outside vendors. There is also annual minimum service charge for engineering clubs/organizations that have a high-volume usage of the machine shop. This charge assists in providing partial reimbursement for supplies, tooling, machine repairs, or any other associated maintenance.

Estimations

All work orders submitted will be evaluated based on priority. Additionally, final charges and estimations will be based on work size and equipment utilized. For full estimates please contact Boris Solomonov – bsolomonov@vcu.edu or 804-827-4013.

Internal Shop Rates (Labor)

$65.00/hour – CAD, Machining, CNC Programming, Tooling Setup, Etc.
